My Favorite Book

I have read La coscienza di Zeno written by Italo Svevo. The title of the book in English is Zeno's Conscience. It is a psychological novel that explores the thoughts and contradictions of the main character, Zeno Cosini.

The novel is structured as a series of memoirs written by Zeno during his psychoanalysis sessions. Through his memories, the reader discovers his relationship with his father, his addiction to smoking and his complicated love life.
